About EOG Resources

EOG Resources is widely regarded as one of the best-run exploration and production companies in the industry. The company was an early mover in the Eagle Ford Shale, where it holds a dominant position in Karnes and Gonzales counties. EOG also has significant Permian Basin acreage, including positions in the Wolfcamp formation in Irion and Upton counties. EOG operates roughly 7,650 wells in Texas and is known for its data-driven approach to exploration. The company uses proprietary analytics to identify and high-grade drilling locations, resulting in consistently strong well performance. EOG's Eagle Ford wells in Karnes County have been among the most prolific in the play's history. For landmen, EOG leases tend to be well-organized with clear terms. The company's title standards are rigorous, and their division order department is responsive.
