About Diamondback Energy

Diamondback Energy has grown from a small Permian Basin operator into one of the largest independent E&P companies in the country. Through a series of strategic acquisitions including QEP Resources, Guidon Energy, and FireBird Energy, Diamondback assembled a premier acreage position across both the Midland and Delaware basins. The company operates approximately 8,900 wells in Texas, with major positions in Midland, Martin, Howard, and Reeves counties. Diamondback is known for operational efficiency and capital discipline, consistently delivering strong well results while maintaining low per-unit costs. Their completion designs including simul-frac operations have helped set industry benchmarks for speed and cost efficiency. Landmen working in Howard or Midland County will frequently encounter Diamondback leases and assignments, particularly in areas where the company has consolidated formerly fragmented acreage positions.
