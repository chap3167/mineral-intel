About Occidental Petroleum (Oxy)

Occidental Petroleum is a Permian Basin institution. The company has operated in West Texas since the 1950s, building a massive position through organic growth and strategic acquisitions, most notably the landmark 2019 acquisition of Anadarko Petroleum. Oxy operates approximately 9,450 wells in Texas with the largest concentrations in Andrews, Ector, Crane, and Gaines counties. Oxy is unique among Permian operators for its leadership in CO2 enhanced oil recovery. The company is the world's largest operator of CO2 EOR projects and uses the technique extensively on its mature conventional fields. For landmen, Oxy leases are ubiquitous across the Central Basin Platform. The company's legacy position built from former Cities Service, Diamond Shamrock, and Anadarko acreage means title chains in Andrews and Gaines counties almost always involve Occidental at some point.
