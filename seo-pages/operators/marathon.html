About Marathon Oil

Marathon Oil is one of the largest operators in the Eagle Ford Shale, with a core position in Karnes, Gonzales, and Atascosa counties. Marathon operates approximately 5,100 wells in Texas, predominantly in the Eagle Ford's volatile oil window where well economics are strongest. Marathon was an early entrant to the Eagle Ford and has steadily drilled out its position with consistent execution. The company's Karnes County wells are among the most productive in the play, benefiting from optimal reservoir conditions in the oil window. Following ConocoPhillips' acquisition of Marathon Oil in 2024, the combined company has an even larger Eagle Ford position. Landmen working in the Eagle Ford core should expect to encounter Marathon leases and assignments throughout Karnes and Gonzales counties.
