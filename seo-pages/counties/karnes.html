Oil & Gas Activity in Karnes County

Karnes County is the crown jewel of the Eagle Ford Shale. The county sits in the volatile oil window where wells have delivered some of the highest initial production rates and best economics in the entire play. Marathon Oil and EOG Resources hold large acreage positions and have drilled hundreds of wells. Karnes County's Eagle Ford development has generated billions in royalties and transformed the local economy since drilling began in earnest around 2010.
