## Link Building Strategy Notes

**Priority targets (highest value):**
- LandmanLife and The Landman Report — Direct audience match
- AAPL — Authoritative industry organization
- AlternativeTo.net — Captures "Enverus alternative" search traffic
- Product Hunt — Launch visibility and backlinks
- Shale Magazine — Permian Basin audience match

**Content-based link building:**
- Publish data-backed articles that industry blogs want to cite
- Create county and formation-level data visualizations that publications embed
- Offer free data access to university programs in exchange for resource page links
- Write contributed articles for Hart Energy and World Oil using MineralSearch data

**Outreach approach:**
- Lead with value (free data, useful content) rather than link requests
- Engage in forums (LandmanLife) as a helpful community member first
- Offer exclusive data analysis to publications for citation
- Submit to directories with complete, professional profiles
